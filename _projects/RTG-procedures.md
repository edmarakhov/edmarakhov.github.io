### Situation
* RTG spreaders were identified as a lead cause of equipment downtime (using findings from RTG Data Analysis project). 
* Existing Preventive Maintenance (PM) procedures had significant gaps in coverage and improper scheduling, leading to frequent preventable failures.

      <li><strong>Process Optimization:</strong> Applied 5S principles to reorganize maintenance tasks, improving clarity and reducing technician error.</li>

### Result
* **Operational Efficiency:** Created standardized, safe PM protocols used fleet-wide, resulting in a 40% reduction in spreader-related downtime.
* **Continuous Improvement:** Established a repeatable, data-driven framework to guide all future PM development at the terminal.
